Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At FI Investment Visualizer, we take your privacy seriously. This document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by our website and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require user accounts or collect personal identification data to use the visualizer. Any financial values or scenarios you input are processed locally in your browser and are not saved on our servers.

2. Google AdSense and Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements when you visit our website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

3. Log Files

Like many other web sites, we make use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends and administer the site.
