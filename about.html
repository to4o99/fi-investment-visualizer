About FI Investment Visualizer

Welcome to the FI Investment Visualizer! This site was built out of a passion for the Financial Independence, Retire Early (FIRE) movement. Our mission is to help individuals visually map out their compound interest projections, tracking milestones and portfolio sustainability over time.

We believe that clean, accessible financial tools can empower people to take control of their retirement future. This application executes client-side computations to give you instant feedback on your financial freedom timelines without storing your sensitive private financial data anywhere.
