How is this calculated?

The calculation uses monthly compound interest. Your Target Portfolio Size is calculated by taking your desired annual income (Monthly × 12) and dividing it by your Safe Withdrawal Rate. For example, $3,000/mo ($36,000/yr) ÷ 3% = $1,200,000 target.

* This simulation assumes steady returns and does not account for inflation, taxes, or market volatility.
